When You Rent a House You Cannot Change the Structure of The House to fit You

colonization of 21st Century

Are we still masters to our colonialists?

A critical look at the Western influence on African culture shows both good and bad influences. This has made much of African native culture give way to the European culture. Either by design or accident, Africans have imbibed the Western culture and have appropriated it so much that it now becomes almost part and parcel of their lives.It is also important to stress that colonialism distorted and retarded the pace and tempo of cultural growth and trend of civilisation in Africa.Today most of the nations in Africa should not even be called African nations, but western African nations. The language, political ideology, socio-economic structures, education, and everything that makes up a nation, even down to popular culture, do not originate from within these countries. African nations have a total dependency on foreign political philosophies and ideas, and their shifts and movements.

1. Western Culture

Westernism is a coinage from the word westernize which refers to the act of bringing ideas or ways of life that are typical of Western Europe and North America to other countries. Therefore, western culture connotes the knowledge, belief, morals and way of life of the Western world.
Culture is learned,it is not transmitted biologically.It is what defines human beings annd distinguishes them from other human beings. Our colonizers brought with them the brilliant idea of Education
In African Traitional set up,our fore-fathers were pretty much satisfied with how they were before the missionaries came.Then came Education is through education that we learn critical thinking.We get to share tables with kings and dine with presidnts through education.It is through the same white education that we have developed good communication skills,so the question of discrimination based on language is eradicated. Every coin has two sides,we forgot our culture and took after our oppressors.We started seeing our culture as barbaric,yes some were but a good number of them were not.Being modernn and civilized meant us aping the west,the metrics of measuring modernity is the western lens.The civilisations of the peoples, their various cultures and traditions, their religions, political philosophies and institutions, were eroded or even destroyed.

What I'd change about that culture

Africa needs to rise up and realise if not reclaim it's power,the world has convinced us that we are inferior and we believed them.Let us adopt our own unique education system that works for us,let us have political and social structures that conform to our needs and social structure.It's only through building your own house that you are at liberty to keep changing it to fit you.Until then ,the owner of the hose will always remind you of how many seconds it would take to throw you out of the house.

2. The narrative of "Love"

Love in Africann Traditional Society was purely for a parent and a daughter.Western culture creeped it,went right through our brains and convinced us that what we new as love had different types,they grouped love into eight groups.Soon we started believing and before we knew it it was deep within us.There's increased divorce in marriages,early pregnancies,and so many STIs that came as a result.
It was the full right of the parents of the girl and the man to begin to find a wife for their son, and give their daughter to anybody of their choice. The next was for him to wait for them to tell him where and when the marriage would take place.In the 21st Century the work of doing the investigation, finding money and other things is not in the hand of the man. Investigation here means finding out whether the girl's people can give birth without difficulties, a trace of rampant adultery, stealing, and premature death and so on.
Due to cultural change, men find their wives on their own without the consent of their parents. They are not willing to know or investigate things about who to marry; they make decisions on where and when the marriage will hold. Which has often times resulted to regrets, abandonment and even quarrel between the two parties. In Africa, both the living and the dead gather during marriage.

What can we do about it?

I agree arranged marriages are not funny,who wants to get stuck with someone they don't "love" for forever?Well atleast not me. I would advice our generation to go slow on the amount of information we take from the west,let us try to find a point of intersection between our "modern" and our parent's "ancient"

Navigating through life amidst a global pandemic

Covid-19 has not only created a the biggest health crisis but it has also but it has also affected the society in a bigger magnitude.Among those affected are the youths who are currently at home.We all know an idle mind is the devils's workshop. This group of the society needs hanling with care,to help them push through.There are some measures that can be put in place to ensure that their physical,mental and spiritual well being is taken care of.

  • Personal Protective Equipment Making

The first response to navigating through the pandemic,without them all other measures are in vain.The youths can be trained on how to make masks and have them make and supply,this will ensure that they are engaged and not viable to endulging in destructive activities.

  • Psychological Support Group

With the widespread of distress during this pandemic,77.7% of youths are likely to fall into depresion.That's a large number right?A good number have lost their jobs,some are out of school annd teir system to some extent had been programmed to experience a different environment at this time. Change and adjustment is the only thing their system can do now.Some of them work places or learning institutions were their escape from mental abuse or even physical abuse, having to deal with that daily is stressing.Mental health can come in handy during this period and this can be done by forming groups for meet,talks and reassurance.

  • Volunteer

Youths can try and find oppotunities within their communities,If there are old people or people whose immunities are low they can help them out.Help vulnerable people to pick groceries,to pick their medicine.They can help in taking are of children whose parents are maybe in the frontline fighting Corona virus or even providing essential services.

  • Creating awareness

There's information overload about the pandemic and a lot of theories on different platforms.Different age groups have different access to information and different interpretation of the same information. In our communities,the youths can take it upon themselves to filter the infomrmation disseminate the right kind of information.By educating the elderly on what to do and keeping them abreast with the current state of affairs taking care not to overload them or cause anxiety.
